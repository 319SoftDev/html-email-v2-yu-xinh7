EXCITING NEWS - HIGH SCHOOL OF FASHION INDUSTRIES DOCUMENTARY


As our returning students may be aware of, in very exciting news the New York Times Documentary team are partnering with Warner Bros. Studios & Berlanti Productions on the production of a documentary about the High School of Fashion Industries.

The project is moving along as they have hired co-directors for the project - Lisa Cortes and Zac Manuel - and a film producer - Howard Gertler. All three of these filmmakers are well respected in their industry and are huge fans of the High School of Fashion Industries. They plan to come to HSFI on Tuesday, September 17 and Wednesday, September 18 to do some filming.

This filming is a continuation of what they call “developmental filming” that began at the end of last school year. They will be taking this video footage and creating a small reel to go out to streaming services like Netflix, HBO, Apple and Amazon, etc. to sell the project to get the funding to actually make the documentary.

There is no guarantee that the project will happen, but the fact that this developmental filming is taking place is a positive sign. For this developmental filming to take place, we need your families to sign a legal release giving permission for the New York Times Documentary team and Warner Bros. Studios / Berlanti Productions to film you.

We will be sending this permission slip out through the Operoo system so your families can expect an email with this agreement. Those Fashion Families that signed the agreement last year do not have to sign it again.

If your families do not sign the agreement, we will make sure you are not included in the video footage used for the production. If your families sign the agreement, we cannot guarantee you will be filmed and included in the documentary because the documentary will obviously not include the whole school. If you have any questions about this filming process, please do not hesitate to contact me.



SCHEDULE CHANGE



We have updated the procedures for students requesting program changes to your class schedules. To request a program change, HSFI students should connect with your counselor in your grade level Wellness Center. The best time to go see their counselor is during your lunch period.

Here are the Wellness Center locations:
□ 9th Grade: Room 643 (on the 6th floor)
□ 10th Grade: Room 723 (on the 7th floor)
□ 11th Grade: Room 423 (on the 4th floor)
□ 12th Grade: Room 504 (on the 5th floor)

After discussing your current schedule and graduation requirements, counselors can submit the requests to our Programming Office. When the Programming office processes the changes, you will be able to see your updated schedule in the Jupiter Grades system the school day after the change is made.

Counselors will be notified of the changes and will reach out to you and your families to explain what happened with the request.

We prioritize students being scheduled for the correct graduation requirements by grade level and will do our best to accommodate other special requests.
